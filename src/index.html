7 to 8 sentences, describing the whole idea and the workflow

The Horse is a curated collection of leather lifestyle goods, owned and operated by husband and wife Scott and Amy Hawkes in their studio in Sydney, Australia.

Together with a small team, we create classic, go-to pieces designed to be agelessly refined.

People new to the story don’t realise that The Horse was established in 2009 by Scott and our friend Gabrielle as a leather footwear label over a coffee one sunny Sunday afternoon, after seeing a man walk past wearing beautiful brown leather shoes and no socks.

These staple styles, solidly made, quickly became a favourite with the well-heeled.

Working alongside each other as a young married couple on a brand and product we believed in was exciting, but the early days made for some testing times and taught us so many lessons. One of our biggest milestones happened in the Spring of 2013, when The Horse launched ‘The Original’ collection of timepieces, which have since become a mainstay of The Horse brand.

Our design philosophy centres around magnifying what is essential and editing out excess, "buying once, and buying well” and we’re always seeking to refine and expand our collection.

Petit Collage is all about good design. We combine a modern aesthetic with a sustainability criteria to create bold, thoughtful goods that inspire little ones. Lovingly designed in our California studio, Petit Collage products are made to encourage creativity, play, and discovery. a world of goods Our goods are sold in thousands of stores. We like to think of them as happy world travelers—whether at a gift boutique in Amsterdam, a book chain in Australia, or a museum in New York City, they can be found the world over, waiting to meet new families.
